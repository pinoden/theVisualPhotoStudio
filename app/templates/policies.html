Studio Rules

Policies

Please review our policies before booking.

Booking & Cancellations

  • Bookings must be made at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the session start time will receive a full refund.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours of the session are non-refundable.
  • No-shows forfeit the full booking amount with no refund or rescheduling.
  • Rescheduling is permitted with at least 24 hours notice, subject to availability.

Studio Use

  • Please arrive no more than 5 minutes before your scheduled start time.
  • Your booking time includes setup and teardown. Please plan accordingly.
  • Clients are responsible for leaving the studio in the same condition as found.
  • Any damage to props, furniture, or the studio itself will be billed to the client.
  • No smoking, vaping, or open flames inside the studio.
  • Food and drinks are permitted in the studio common area; please use care near props and furniture.
  • The maximum number of people in any studio is 10.

Overtime

  • Sessions running over the booked time will be charged at the standard hourly rate in 30-minute increments.
  • If a subsequent booking follows yours, overtime may not be possible. Please communicate promptly.

Props & Equipment

  • All studio props and furniture are to be used gently and returned to their original placement.
  • You are welcome to bring your own equipment and props.
  • Please do not move or remove built-in fixtures without prior approval.

Respectful Environment

  • We are a safe and inclusive space. Disrespectful or inappropriate behavior will result in immediate removal without refund.
  • Content of an explicit or illegal nature is strictly prohibited.
  • Please keep noise levels considerate of neighboring suites.
Questions?

If you have any questions about our policies, please contact us before booking.
